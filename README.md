# 📊 Retail Business Analytics | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

> End-to-end Retail Business Analytics project using Python, SQL, Power BI, and DAX to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and strategic business recommendations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Businesses often experience increasing sales without a proportional increase in profits. This project investigates the reasons behind this trend by analyzing retail transaction data and identifying opportunities to improve profitability.

The analysis focuses on answering real business questions rather than simply creating visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company observed that:

- Sales are increasing.
- Profit growth is inconsistent.
- Some products generate significant losses.
- Discounts may be reducing profitability.

The objective is to identify:

- Which products drive revenue?
- Which products generate losses?
- Which customers contribute the most value?
- Which regions perform best?
- How can the company improve profitability?

## 💡 Business Insights

Some important insights discovered during the analysis include:

- Technology contributes the highest revenue.
- Certain products generate consistent losses.
- Profitability varies significantly across regions.
- High discounts often reduce overall profitability.
- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total sales.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Review discount policies for low-margin products.
- Focus marketing efforts on profitable customer segments.
- Investigate products with recurring losses.
- Improve inventory planning for high-demand categories.
- Expand operations in high-performing regions.
